Welcome

Welcome to Drew Center Pharmacy

Retail Pharmacy In East Palo Alto, California

Drew Center Pharmacy is a 50-year-old pharmacy that provides professional services to a diverse and underserved minority community in East Palo Alto, California.

We offer a wide selection of prescription medications as well as non-prescription drugs, vitamins, and nutritional supplements — with access to medical supplies, equipment, and healthcare products. In addition, we have many special gifts, ethnic collectibles, fashion jewelry, and so much more.

We also provide copying services, color photo services, faxing services, and utility payment services. Our goal is to offer you a hassle-free, quick, and complete shopping experience with the hopes that your visit will fulfill your healthcare and personal needs.

Every day, we are inspired to serve your healthcare and personal needs — and we hope you will become our best promoter. Help Drew help you!

Our Story

50 Years Serving
East Palo Alto

Drew Center Pharmacy was founded in 1974 as part of the Charles R. Drew Medical Center — named for the pioneering physician who made modern blood transfusion possible. That legacy of dedicated community service set the standard from day one.

In 1983, Dr. Arthur H. Lim, Pharm.D. took independent ownership, bringing over 40 years of personal expertise and community trust to every prescription filled and every question answered.

Today, Drew Center remains one of the last independent community pharmacies in the area — a trusted neighbor, healthcare partner, and community institution.
